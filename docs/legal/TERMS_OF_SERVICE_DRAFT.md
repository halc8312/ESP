# Terms of Service Draft

This draft requires legal review before public release.

## Service Scope

ESP helps authorized users manage sourced product records, public catalog pages, and export files. Users are responsible for ensuring they have rights to use imported product data, descriptions, images, and marketplace content.

## Account Access

Public signup is disabled by default for production. Accounts should be invited or administrator-created unless a public signup launch has been approved.

## Acceptable Use

Users must not upload or import unlawful content, credentials, payment card data, malware, or data they are not authorized to process.

## Scraping and External Services

Users are responsible for complying with source-site terms, robots policies, rate limits, and applicable laws when using scraping or monitoring features.

## Disclaimers

Pricing, availability, exchange rates, and generated exports may be inaccurate or delayed. Users should verify data before publishing listings or sending customer offers.

## Termination and Data Removal

Define suspension, account termination, data export, and deletion procedures before public launch.
